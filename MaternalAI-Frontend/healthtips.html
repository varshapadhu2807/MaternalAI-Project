💡 Maternal Health Tips

Expert-guided wellness tips to support a healthy pregnancy journey for mother and baby.

🥗

Nutrition

Eat iron-rich foods, fruits, vegetables, whole grains and maintain a balanced diet.

💊

Prenatal Vitamins

Take folic acid and prenatal supplements regularly as recommended by healthcare professionals.

🚶

Safe Exercise

Light activities like walking and stretching can support circulation and wellness.

😴

Proper Rest

Maintain good sleep habits and reduce unnecessary stress during pregnancy.

🩺

Regular Checkups

Attend prenatal appointments and monitor pregnancy progress regularly.

💧

Hydration

Drink enough water daily to support maternal health and energy levels.
